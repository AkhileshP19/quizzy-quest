Unleash Your Knowledge Adventure!

Welcome to QuizzyQuest, a quiz application that tests your knowledge about science & technology to history & mythology and from celebreties & arts to sports & politics. Be ready to face truly diverse set of questions & showcase your talent.

Why choose QuizzyQuest?

  • Play for free, no sign up required
  • Diverse quiz categories
  • Varying difficulty level
  • Timmed quizzes for added excitement
  • Detailed summary & score after quiz

How it works?

  • 1. Click on Get Started
  • 2. Select the no. of questions
  • 3. Select quiz category
  • 4. Select difficulty level
  • 5. Start the quiz

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is the app free ?

Yes

Do I have to register or sign up to attempt the quiz ?

No, its absolutely free

How are scores calculated ?

For each correct answer you get 1 point and no points are deducted for any wrong answer.

Is it a timed quiz ?

Yes, for each question you get 30 seconds to answer.

Can I customize the quiz ?

Yes, of course. You can choose any of the category from about 22 categories. You can also choose the difficulty level among easy, medium and difficult. You can also specify the number of questions.

Is it possible to play a quiz involving all categories ?

Yes, it is possible. Just click on Start Quiz without modifying the category and difficulty level. You can modify the no. of questions. This will start a randomized quiz.
